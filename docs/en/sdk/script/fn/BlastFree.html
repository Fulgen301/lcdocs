BlastFree

Category: Landscape
Since engine version: 4.6.5.0 CP

Description

Creates a hole in the landscape in the shape of a circle, as if it was blasted. Materials are converted into their material items, for example gold to gold lumps.

Syntax

voidBlastFree (int말X, int말Y, int말Radius, int말CausedBy);

Parameters

iX:
X coordinate / X offset of the center
iY:
Y coordinate / Y offset of the center
iRadius:
Radius of the dug free area.
iCausedBy:
Number of the player who caused the "explosion" plus one.
See also: DigFree, DigFreeRect, FreeRect
